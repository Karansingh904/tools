How to Compress Images

  1. Upload your image by dragging and dropping or clicking the upload area
  2. Adjust the compression level and output format settings
  3. Optionally enable resizing to reduce dimensions
  4. Click "Compress Image" to process your file
  5. Download the optimized image

Why Compress Images?

Image compression helps:

  • Save storage space
  • Enhance user experience
  • Boost SEO rankings (faster pages rank higher)
